About the Faculty

The Faculty of Engineering and Technology at Birzeit University empowers future engineers through a forward-thinking educational environment that integrates theoretical excellence with real-world application.

We inspire innovation, critical thinking, and leadership by fostering a culture that merges rigorous academics with cutting-edge lab experiences, hands-on learning, and community-driven projects.

Our Specializations

From smart infrastructure to advanced computing, our departments provide a strong foundation and modern tools for disciplines like Computer Engineering, Electrical Systems, Civil Development, and AI-driven Automation.

Beyond the Classroom

We believe learning extends beyond textbooks. Our students engage in real-world simulations, hackathons, and inter-university competitions, applying their skills to create tangible, impactful solutions.
